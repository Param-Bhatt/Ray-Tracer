  2. Issues in Coding


    • The first and the main issue we found was in the idea and writing the algorithm for our tracer. It took us a lot of time and research to understand the complete geometry involved behind the working of a simple tracer. We misunderstood some things in between and hence were setback in the middle too. However , we finally did understand the complete geometry as explained in project and wrote out the algorithm for it.

    • Another issue we faced was in implementing the entire algorithms together as one.Some of the issues include background being white led to the shadows being white . We also generated shades using diffusion coefficient for colouring our objects accordingly ( This is called Lambert Shading ) and also used Blinn-Phong shading for illumiating the spot where light is concentrated the most.

    • Often, we had to readjust our code and update it accordingly as some of the light rays would not illuminate one particular spot, or illuminated one spot only and ignored all the rest. However, when it all ended and fell into place as one program , we really loved our output obtained and our code runs successfully as of now.
